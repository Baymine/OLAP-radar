- **Publication date:** 2026-03-02  
- **Research problem:** General-purpose OLAP engines pay unavoidable overheads for flexibility, including schema interpretation, abstraction layers, and generic execution paths. More broadly, it raises a strategic question for the field: whether future analytical systems should be maintained as reusable engines, or increasingly synthesized on demand for known workloads, hardware, and data shapes.
